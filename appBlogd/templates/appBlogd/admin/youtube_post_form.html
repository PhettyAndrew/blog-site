How to post a Youtube Video

Follow the instructions below for what is required in the Url Field

  1. Go to Youtube
  2. Click on the Video you want to view
  3. Check the link on the url bar
    You will see a link like this https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-7PTBFlhIds&t=1s
  4. Only copy the short address to the video
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-7PTBFlhIds&t=1s
  5. In our case the short address to the video is -7PTBFlhIds&t=1s
  6. Paste the Short address in the Url Field
  7. Fill the remaining fields and Submit
*Be sure to follow the instructions for a successful post*
